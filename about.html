About Us

Learn more about the Burnley Community Arts Festival here!

The Burnley Community Arts Festival is a celebration of the incredible creativity that comes from our town. We believe art is for everyone, and so our mission is to provide a platform for our local artists to showcase their talent and bring our community together through the accessible events that we provide every year.
Our work goes beyond a single weekend event. By bringing people together, we aim to strengthen community bonds, while creating a lasting economic opportunity for Burnley's creative sector. The festival is also a catalyst for cultural growth, allowing us to inspire the next generation of artists and ensuring our town becomes a beautiful place for creativity.

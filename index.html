About the Festival

The Burnley Community Arts Festival is a new annual event designed to showcase the incredible artistic talent within our town. We aim to bring people together through a celebration of visual arts, music, theatre, and performance.

Whether you're an artist, a performer, or an art enthusiast, there's something for you. Join us in making Burnley a hub for creativity and culture.

Opening Times

  • Friday: 5:00 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Saturday & Sunday: 11:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Entry is free for all daytime events.
Evening performances (6pm and onwards) will require a ticket.

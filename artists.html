Our Artists

Explore our incredible artists through these great images captured by members of the community.

Rain Whalley

Rain Whalley is our extraordinaire when it comes to making and decorating lanterns and lamps for our community thanks to their amazing skills.

Vincent Mercer

Vincent Mercer is an incredibly skillful artist when it comes to creating hats and other types of accesories, allowing him to be popular within our community.

Chie Hori

Chie Hori is one of the best behind a camera, winning many different competitions around the world and has now settled in within our community.

Bartlomiej Wojda

When it comes to arts and crafts, Bartlomiej Wojda is fantastic, helping young people when it comes to exploring the concept of art while allowing them to make their own creations.

Iwakura Lain

Iwakura Lain is a bit shy, but their talent for capturing beautiful images is truly impressive, with 105 competition wins both globally and in our own country.

David Szymanski

David Szymanski is an incredible being when it comes to using watercolour to capture feelings and descriptions within each and every one of us.

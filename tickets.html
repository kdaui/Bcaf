Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a ticket for daytime events?

No, all daytime events are completely free and open to the public.

Can I buy tickets on the door?

Subject to availability. We highly recommend booking in advance online to guarantee your entry, as evening shows are expected to sell out.

Are there concessions available?

Yes, we offer a 50% discount for students and under-18s. Valid ID will be required upon entry.
